Smart Lighting

INTRODUCTION

Lights for every activity

Smart lighting can make your home more comfortable, safe, and enjoyable while saving energy. With Shelly, you can easily control your lights remotely, set smart schedules and timers, reducing energy waste and lowering your electricity bills. Additionally, lighting accounts for 7% of a home's carbon footprint, so making your lighting smarter is a simple way to help the environment too.
